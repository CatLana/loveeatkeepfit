# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Love. Eat. Keep Fit. website and services.

## What Data We Collect

- **Form Submissions:** When you fill out our intake or contact forms, we collect the information you provide (such as name, email, health and lifestyle data, and any other details you submit).

## How We Use Your Data

- To respond to your inquiries and provide coaching services.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to provide our services (e.g., email delivery providers).

## Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
